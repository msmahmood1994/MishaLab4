Misha Mahmood

My name is Misha Mahmood, and I am a graphic designer from Huntington Beach, California. I specialize in editorial, print, digital, packaging, social media, and user interface design.

I completed my B.F.A. in graphic design from the Savannah College of Art and Design. I am studying part-time at the University of Southern California to earn an M.S. in Integrated Design, Business, and Technology. I have worked in various industries such as advertising, wine, cannabis, and healthcare.
